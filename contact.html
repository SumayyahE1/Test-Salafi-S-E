Frequently Asked Questions

How can I suggest a resource to be added?

You can suggest resources by emailing us at resources@salafisearch.com with the details of the resource and why you believe it should be included.

What criteria do you use for selecting resources?

We verify that resources follow the Salafi methodology, provide authentic Islamic knowledge, and are from trusted scholars or students of knowledge.

Can I request a feature for the search engine?

Yes! We welcome feature suggestions. Please email us with your ideas and we'll consider them for future updates.

How often is the resource list updated?

We regularly review and update our resource list to ensure all links are working and to add new authentic sources.
